How to Play

1.

Hit the target number at the top by combining numbers and operators.

2.

You must use all 7 number nodes, each exactly once.

3.

Tap numbers and operators to build an equation. Math order-of-operations applies (× ÷ before + −).

4.

You get 3 attempts. After each miss you'll see how far off (and which way) you were.

5.

Daily Crypt: one shared puzzle a day — solve it to keep your streak and climb the ranks. Endless: grind points for themes.
